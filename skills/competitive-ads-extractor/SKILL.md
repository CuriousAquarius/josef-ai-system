# Competitive Ads Extractor

This skill extracts your competitors' ads from ad libraries and analyzes what's working — the problems they're highlighting, use cases they're targeting, and copy/creative that's resonating.

## When to Use This Skill

- Researching competitor ad strategies before launching your own campaign
- Finding inspiration for Serra marketing or any product ads
- Understanding how other chatbot/SaaS companies position themselves
- Identifying pain points the market responds to
- Planning ad campaigns with proven concepts

## How to Use

### Basic Extraction
```
Extract all current ads from [Competitor Name] on Facebook Ad Library
```

### Messaging Analysis
```
Get all ads from [Competitor] focusing on their messaging about [specific problem]. What pain points are they highlighting?
```

### Multi-Competitor Comparison
```
Extract ads from these competitors: [list]. Compare their approaches and tell me what's working.
```

### Platform-Specific
```
Get LinkedIn ads from [Competitor] and analyze their B2B positioning strategy
```

## What This Skill Does

1. **Extracts Ads** — Scrapes ads from Facebook Ad Library, LinkedIn, etc.
2. **Captures Screenshots** — Saves visual copies of all ads
3. **Analyzes Messaging** — Identifies problems, use cases, and value propositions
4. **Categorizes Ads** — Groups by theme, audience, or format
5. **Identifies Patterns** — Finds common successful approaches
6. **Provides Recommendations** — Explains what to test in your own campaigns

## Output Includes

- Total ad count and active campaigns
- Primary themes and message categories
- Key problems competitors are highlighting
- Successful headline and copy patterns
- Visual/creative format breakdown (video vs static, layouts)
- Audience targeting insights
- Actionable recommendations for your own ads
- Files saved: screenshots, analysis report, CSV of ad copy

## Advanced Workflows

### Trend Tracking
```
Compare [Competitor]'s ads from Q1 vs Q2. What messaging has changed?
```

### Industry Benchmarks
```
Show me ad patterns across the top 5 [industry] companies. What problems do they all focus on?
```

### Campaign Planning
```
I'm launching Serra to [target audience]. Extract ads from similar chatbot tools and tell me what messaging angles are working.
```

## Legal & Ethical Guidelines

✓ Use only for research and inspiration
✓ Respect intellectual property
✓ Adapt successful patterns into original creative
✗ Don't copy ads directly or plagiarize copy

## Tips for Success

- Check monthly — competitor messaging evolves
- Look at adjacent industries too, not just direct competitors
- Note what's *absent* — gaps in competitor messaging are opportunities
- Test 2-3 inspired concepts before scaling any one approach
